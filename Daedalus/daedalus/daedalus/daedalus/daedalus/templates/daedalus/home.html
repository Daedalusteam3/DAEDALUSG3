What does Daedalus do?

Daedalus combines demand simulation, optimization models (PuLP), GTFS data and computer vision to offer a complete tool for public transport analysis and management.

Capabilities

  • Discrete-time demand simulation.
  • Automatic frequency adjustment by district.
  • Intelligent ad selection system based on detected audience.
  • Real-time updates via Kafka.
  • Structured storage in Supabase.

Integrated platform

The advertising module detects people at each stop through a camera, classifies age, gender and expected product, and selects the most suitable ad in real time.
